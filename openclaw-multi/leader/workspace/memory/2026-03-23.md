## 选定方法
- **名称**：Diff-LM-GZSL (Language-Driven Latent Diffusion for Continuous Semantic Alignment in Zero-Shot Fault Diagnosis)

- **Baseline**：FDAT, SCE, GLA-ZSL, FAGAN, SRWGAN, VAEGAN-AR, FREE, GZSLCFD, DP-CDDPM-AC
